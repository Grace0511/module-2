2 tablespoons flour Add butter to skillet, as soon as butter melts whisk in flour. Continue whisking until flour is fully mixed with butter. Then cook for about a minute to get rid of the flour taste.
1 12oz canned evaporated milk Slowly add evaporated milk a little at the time, followed by the half and half ; you do not want the mixture to form any lumps. Simmer for about 3-5 minutes until mixture thickens slightly
½ cup half and half Add seasonings, onion and garlic powder, creole seasoning and cayenne pepper.
½ -1 tablespoon onion powder Bring to a simmer and let it simmer gently for about 2 minutes.
2 teaspoons garlic powder Stir in the cheeses (reserve some as toppings later), and continue stirring until everything’s melted and evenly combined and, smooth. Salt & pepper, to taste.
½ -1 teaspoon creole seasoning Then add the cooked pasta to the pot, stir to evenly incorporate.
¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper Transfer the pasta mixture into a the pan or into a lightly greased 2-qt. baking dish; top with remaining cheese.
½ cup mozzarella cheese , grated Bake at 375 Degrees F° for 20 minutes or until golden and bubbly

Pizza

Ingredients Directions
1 pizza crust Prepare pizza dough through step 5, including preheating the oven to 475°F (246°C). Cover the shaped dough lightly with plastic wrap and allow it to rest as the oven preheats.
1/2 cup pizza sauce To prevent the pizza toppings from making your pizza crust soggy, brush the shaped dough lightly with olive oil. Using your fingers, push dents into the surface of the dough to prevent bubbling. Top the dough evenly with pizza sauce, then add the cheese, ham, pineapple, and bacon.
1 and 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese Bake pizza for 12-15 minutes. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil, if desired. Slice hot pizza and serve immediately.

Tacos

Ingredients Directons
2 teaspoons vegetable oil Heat the oil in a large pan over medium high heat.
1 pound lean ground beef Add the ground beef and break up with a spatula.
1/2 cup onion finely chopped Add the onion to the pan.
1 tablespoon chili powder Cook, stirring occasionally, until beef is done and onion is soft, 5-6 minutes. Drain off any excess fat.
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder Add the chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, red pepper flakes, oregano, smoked paprika, cumin, salt and pepper to the pan. Stir to coat the meat in the seasonings.
1/4 teaspoon onion powder Add the tomatoes to the pan and simmer for 2-3 minutes
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional) Spoon the beef into the taco shells and add toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes and cheese. Serve immediately.

Apple crisp

Ingredients Directons
6 medium (6 cups) tart cooking apples, peeled, cored, sliced Heat oven to 375°F.
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar Place apples into ungreased 8-inch square baking dish.
3/4 cup uncooked old-fashioned oats Combine brown sugar, oats, flour and cinnamon in bowl; cut in butter with pastry blender or fork until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle sugar mixture over apples. Bake 25-35 minutes or until apples are tender and topping is golden brown.

Angel Food Cake Fruit Trifle

Ingredients Directions
1 bundt pan cooked angel food cake homemade or store-bought In a medium saucepan over medium heat, bring 2 cups of the milk to a boil.
5 - 6 cups fresh fruit for topping While the milk is heating, combine sugar, cornstarch, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Gradually whisk in the remaining 2/3 cup milk, stirring until no clumps remain. Add the egg and whisk until smooth
2 cups heavy whipping cream Once the milk is boiling, slowly add it to the sugar mixture, stirring constantly
1/2 cup powdered sugar Return the entire mixture back to the saucepan, stirring constantly until it comes to a simmer. Cook it for one more minute, until thickened. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract
2 teaspoons vanilla extract (If the pudding has any lumps you can strain it through a fine mesh strainer.) Transfer pudding to a heat proof bowl and cover the pudding with plastic wrap, pressing it directly on the surface of the pudding. Refrigerate until chilled completely. Store covered in the fridge
2 2/3 cups whole milk divided Cut or tear the angel food cake into chunks. Prepare fruit by washing and slicing it as necessary
1/2 cup granulated sugar Make the fresh whipped cream by adding the heavy cream to a large mixing bowl. Beat with electric mixers on high speed for a few seconds. Add powdered sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks. Stir in vanilla
1/4 cup cornstarch To fill the trifle, add several chunks of angel food cake to the bottom of the dish. Dollop several spoonfuls of pudding around the cake. Add a variety of the fruit, followed by a few dollops of fresh whipped cream. Repeat process until the trifle is filled. (I don't bother making "perfect" layers)
